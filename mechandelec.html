Mechanical Repairs

Mechanical repairs focus on the physical components of your vehicle, including the engine, transmission, brakes, and suspension. Our skilled technicians are equipped to handle everything from routine maintenance to complex repairs. We diagnose issues accurately, using advanced tools and techniques to restore your vehicle to optimal condition. Whether it’s a brake pad replacement or a complete engine rebuild, we ensure that your car performs at its best.

Electrical Repairs

In today’s vehicles, electrical systems play a vital role in functionality and safety. Our electrical repair services cover everything from battery and alternator issues to complex computer diagnostics. We can troubleshoot and fix problems with wiring, sensors, and lighting systems, ensuring that all electrical components operate seamlessly. With our expertise, you can trust that your vehicle’s electrical system will be reliable and efficient.

01
Battery Services

Testing and Replacement: We test battery performance and replace weak or dead batteries to keep your vehicle running smoothly.

Battery Terminal Cleaning: Corroded terminals can affect performance; we provide cleaning services to ensure proper connections.

02
Starter and Alternator Repair

Starter Diagnostics: If your vehicle doesn’t start, we diagnose starter issues and provide necessary repairs or replacements.

Alternator Repair: We ensure your alternator is charging properly to prevent battery drain and electrical system failures.

03
Electrical System Diagnostics

Comprehensive Checks: Our team conducts thorough diagnostics on your vehicle’s electrical systems, identifying issues with wiring, fuses, and components.

Repair Services: We repair or replace malfunctioning electrical parts to restore functionality and safety.

04
Lighting and Signal Repairs

Headlight and Taillight Services: We replace burnt-out bulbs, repair wiring, and ensure your lighting system is compliant with safety standards.

Signal System Repair: Our technicians inspect and repair turn signals and hazard lights to ensure clear communication on the road.

05
Advanced Computer Diagnostics

ECU Programming: We handle issues related to your vehicle’s engine control unit (ECU) with expert programming and diagnostics.

Sensor Repair and Replacement: From oxygen sensors to speed sensors, we diagnose and replace faulty components for optimal vehicle performance.
