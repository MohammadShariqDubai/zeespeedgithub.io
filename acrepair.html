AC Repairs Services

At Zee Speed Auto Repair LLC, we know how crucial a functioning air conditioning system is for your comfort, especially in Dubai’s heat. Our comprehensive A/C repair services are designed to diagnose and resolve a variety of issues, ensuring you enjoy a cool and pleasant driving experience. Here’s a detailed overview of our A/C repair offerings:

01
Comprehensive A/C Inspections

Thorough Diagnostics: We conduct detailed inspections of your A/C system, assessing all key components such as the compressor, condenser, evaporator, and refrigerant levels.

Quick Issue Identification: Utilizing advanced diagnostic tools, we can quickly pinpoint issues affecting your A/C performance, allowing for prompt repairs.

02
Refrigerant Recharge

Refrigerant Level Check: If your A/C blows warm air, low refrigerant levels may be the cause. We ensure proper levels are maintained for optimal cooling.

Safe Recharge: Our technicians safely recharge your A/C system with the correct type and amount of refrigerant to restore efficiency.

03
Component Repairs and Replacements

Compressor Repair and Replacement: The compressor is essential for A/C operation. We diagnose and repair or replace it to ensure proper cooling performance.

Condenser and Evaporator Services: We handle repairs to the condenser and evaporator, addressing leaks and blockages to maintain efficient airflow.

04
Airflow and Blower Motor Services

Airflow Diagnostics: Reduced airflow may indicate clogged filters or a failing blower motor. We inspect and address these issues to restore optimal performance.

: If the blower motor is malfunctioning, we provide replacement services to ensure proper airflow throughout your vehicle.

05
System Cleaning and Maintenance

Cleaning Services: We perform thorough cleaning of the A/C system, including ducts and evaporator, to eliminate odors and contaminants for a fresh cabin.

Preventive Maintenance: Our maintenance packages include regular inspections and cleaning, helping to prevent future A/C issues and prolong system life.

06
Leak Detection and Repair

Leak Testing: We conduct leak detection tests to identify refrigerant leaks in your A/C system, ensuring no loss of efficiency.

Timely Repairs: Once leaks are detected, we provide prompt repair services to restore your A/C system’s functionality and performance.
